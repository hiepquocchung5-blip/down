Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Slopara ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and download portal.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better gaming experience for all our players. This includes:

  • Account Profiles: When you register, we collect credentials such as phone numbers, device IDs, or display usernames to sync your profile.
  • Gameplay Activity: We record virtual coin balances, daily reward logs, and cabinet gameplay data to prevent synchronization errors and ensure fair leaderboard ranks.
  • Device Logs: We log standard telemetry (operating system version, screen resolution) to optimize app frame-rates and assets dynamically.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used strictly to support our game platform's operation:

  • To secure and authenticate your user account logins.
  • To run the local synchronized profile systems and prevent data loss.
  • To calculate local leaderboards and award daily coin streaks correctly.
  • To improve security, analyze error dumps, and harden the database connections against scraping.

3. Data Storage & Safety

All profile data is protected using standard TLS/SSL encryption methods during transit. We do not store or process real financial details (credit cards or bank details) on our systems. Virtual coin transactions are performed via secure localized bot integrations and double-checked for session validity.

4. Cookies & Web Tracking

Our download portal uses minimal cache cookies to store user preference options such as dark mode configurations. No cross-site advertisement scripts are injected, keeping your browsing session clean.

5. Children's Privacy

This App contains simulated arcade mechanics and is intended only for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

6. Contact Us

For any questions about your data security or profile privacy, please reach out to our team at support@slopara.com.
